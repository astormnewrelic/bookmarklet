This crude bookmarklet scans the current page for <a/>nchors with a data-url attribute, looks for a URL slug in that data, and adds an href to the <a/>. It makes the assumption that slugs can always be appended to https://nerdlife.datanerd.us/new-relic/ to get a full URL. By turning these "data url <a/>nchors into real hyperlinks, we get back things like "open in new window/tab".
